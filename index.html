P5.JS

FISHING, INTERACTIVE NET ART, 2019.


This is a net art piece which is made with p5.js program to create a fishing game. The game is done by clicking to the screen. If the mouse is above the school fish, it'll turn to bubble. If the mouse is below the fish, it'll turn to the sushi.

Final Project

B.L.I.N.K, DIGITAL VIDEO, 2:00, 2019.


My final project for art74 class is a stop motion video. This video is about the contrast between pink and black. I choose my makeups to represent the two colors. There are 3 parts in the video: pink, black, blink (which is combination of pink and black, but also means "blink"). The video also has the relationship and interaction between the objects. To do so, I add some animation effects and sound to make the objects alive.
